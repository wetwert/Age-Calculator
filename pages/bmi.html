Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?
Body Mass Index compares weight to height to estimate weight status.
What is a healthy BMI?
Generally 18.5–24.9 is normal; 25–29.9 overweight; 30+ obese.
Is BMI accurate for athletes?
May overestimate body fat in muscular individuals.
